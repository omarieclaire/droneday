Drone 2020 is almost here. As always, we welcome any kind of celebration you want to make. Livestreams, solo drones, balcony drones: all welcome.

We're also looking into online tools/platforms for bringing our drones together.

Below are some considerations, inspirations, and things people are planning

Consider:

  • how to gather when we can’t actually gather?
  • how to include curious beginners?
  • is there something we can do to bring these drones together?
  • what can we do in the time we have? (who wants to help?)

Ideas:

  • make a virtual world where people can wander from drone to drone? (rooms?)
  • make an online map with dots - explore the drone world?
  • chained livestreams - some love live streaming, others find it lonely
  • make a compilation site where droners can submit music, visuals, other
  • make a mix site where guests can live-mix the sound?
  • make a site where you can record your voice & it is drawn out into a drone that you can share?
  • offline drones, send them to each other through the soil and air?

Things people are planning for this year

  • a long distance drone call theme. Setting up in the woods having folks spread out conversing through drone (whitehorse) 
  • an open call for contributions to a special Drone playlist which someone will stream for 24hrs (audio or video file). Run the playlist on a loop if it is less than 24hrs long, or extend it beyond the 24hrs if it runs longer. 
  • CJLO would love to air a DD broadcast if we do one and attempt to get other campus stations
