Droooooone


We'd be delighted to drone with you. Send us a link to your drone day celebration and we'll add it to the site. This could be a livestream link, a hand-drawn poster, or location on a map, or any kind of link that communicates your celebration.

Saturday May 28 is the 9th annual Drone Day.

Everyone is invited to take part. If you can make a sustained sound, join us! Celebrate in a way that is meaningful to you, invent your own rituals. Make long sounds. Sing to tiny green plants? Plant a seed? Play with sound? Sit on your balcony, on your stoop, lean out your window, and drone with us.

How do I make a drone?

Can you make a sound? Can you make it longer? Just make any sustained, extended, or repeating tones. All sounds welcome: drones made with your voice, drones made by appliances, bagpipe drones, homemade drones, synth drones, anything that is long and vibrates in your bones.

How do I celebrate?

At any time on Drone Day make long sounds that feel good in your ears and see where it takes you. Beginner (‘I have no idea what I’m doing’) celebrations welcome. Professional musicians welcome. New rituals and experiments welcome. Use your imagination and do what feels good for you. If you send us a livestream link, we'll share it.

Why?

Now more than ever, we need to make time for connection. Every spring we reach out to each other across impossible geographic distances with our looong tones. Together we are many small creatures wrapping our shared world in one long drone.
